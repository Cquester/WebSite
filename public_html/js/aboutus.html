Our History



Established in 1994.

German Car Service has been providing Mercedes Benz owners an option to get dealer quality service without the dealer price. Even though we have only been established since 1994, our mechanic's have been working on Mercedes for most of their life.
Not only do we have the experience but we also have the tools. With all the modern computer technology that is in car's now a days you need a special scan tool to be able to not only look at the codes a car gives but also to monitor different aspects of the car. So next time you think about going to a repair shop please give us a call and we can show you why you want us to service your car.
